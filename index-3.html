Advantages of Contract drafting (DIY)

Saves time

The usage of the templatized version of Agreements reduces and saves times as there is no time invested in the negotiations, drafting and approvals. With this you accelerate the growth of your business by saving time in formalizing legal relationships.

Easy to understand

Technical terms in any agreement leads to inconsistencies and also creates a room for seeking professional advice. The DIY agreements are drafted in a user- friendly manner and have a very easy and comprehensible language which can be understood even by a layman.

Formalize legal relationship

The Agreement will legalize the transaction between the parties and will eliminate the need for customized agreements for every individual. This eases out the process of legalizing and formalizing the legal relationship between the parties

Reduced transaction cost

The DIY agreements save time and transaction cost as they limit the customization process and the need for customization is eliminated. With DIY agreements, the cost for outsourcing the drafting process to a specialist is also reduced.
